Instructor-led

Traditional instructor-led workshops costs many thousands of dollars. Compared to pre-recorded workshops that we buy online for little money, we expect greater value. That's not always the fact, but the reality.

We took the best of both worlds, and combined them in one workshop. The result is an instructor-led workshop for 50€. You can still ask questions, interact with each other, or just listen to the instructor.

Small Groups

For many people, it's more fun to learn in small groups. It's also easier to ask questions. The same is true for the instructor. The instructor will learn about your challenges in much more detail, and can give you better answers. This workshop has a limit of 5 participants. Changing Focus Each Week

Azure API Management has many great features. Each week we will focus on one feature in more detail than on others. We will always cover the basics, so everyone in this workshop will be on the same page. In one workshop we might focus on Logging, in the next workshop we will investigate the Developer Portal.
